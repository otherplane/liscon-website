Covid Measures

Health and safety are a priority at LisCon.

All ticket holders must have EU vaccine certificates or a negative PCR test result valid for 72 hours or a negative Antigen test result valid for 48 hours or a valid recovery certificate to gain entry to the event.

Masks are mandatory and must be worn indoors at all times.

Liscon has ensured all appropriate measures as set by the Portuguese health authorities in October are adhered to.

Any attendee disrespectful of sanitary measures will be refused the right of entry.

What if I took a PCR or Antigen test outside of the EU?

If you have taken a PCR Test 72h before or Antigen Test 48h before the event, we will accept it. Vaccination cards from outside the EU will not be accepted.

What if I dont have an EU vaccine certificate or PCR/Antigen test?

There are several pharmacies in Lisbon to do an Antigen test, results take typically 15 mins. You can find the list of pharmacies here accepted by the European Union Health Committee.
